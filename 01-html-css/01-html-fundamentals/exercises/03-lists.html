How to Make Coffee

  1. Boil water
  2. Grind coffee beans
  3. Place a filter in the coffee maker
  4. Add ground coffee to the filter
  5. Pour hot water over the grounds
  6. Let it brew for a few minutes
  7. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
