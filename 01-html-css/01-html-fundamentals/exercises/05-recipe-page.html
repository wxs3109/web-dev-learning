Chocolate Chip Cookies

These classic chocolate chip cookies are crispy on the edges and chewy in the center. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour and baking soda, then gradually blend into the creamed mixture.
  5. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until edges are nicely browned.

Tips

Important: Do not overbake! The cookies will continue to cook on the hot cookie sheet after you take them out of the oven.

"The secret to perfect cookies is to chill the dough for at least an hour before baking." - Grandma's Tip
